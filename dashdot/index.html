01  /  TRANSMIT

SEND WITH LIGHT

Your camera flash becomes a Morse code transmitter. Every dot and dash pulses in sync with the vibration motor.

TRANSMITTING
— · ·
CAMERA FLASH
  1. 01

    TYPE YOUR MESSAGE

    Enter any text using A–Z and 0–9. A live Morse preview scrolls below your input.

  2. 02

    SET TRANSMISSION SPEED

    Drag the speed slider from 60 ms (fast, ~20 WPM) to 200 ms (slow, ~6 WPM). Match your receiver's setting.

  3. 03

    PRESS TRANSMIT

    The camera flash pulses each dot and dash. The vibration motor syncs perfectly with every signal. Tap STOP to cancel instantly.

02  /  RECEIVE

READ WITH THE CAMERA

Point your camera at the sender's flash. DashDot samples brightness ~12 times per second and decodes the pattern in real time.

  1. 01

    OPEN RX MODE

    Tap RX ▶ in the top-right corner of the main screen.

  2. 02

    CALIBRATE

    Cover the camera (SAMPLE DARK), then point at the sender's torch (SAMPLE BRIGHT). DashDot sets the detection threshold automatically.

  3. 03

    PRESS RECEIVE

    Decoded text appears letter by letter as the sender transmits. A blinking cursor shows the receiver is live.

03  /  FEATURES

EVERYTHING YOU NEED

⚡ TX
FLASH TRANSMISSION
Camera torch pulses International Morse Code with precise 1:3:7 timing. Up to 20 WPM at maximum speed.
〰 TX
VIBRATION SYNC
The haptic motor mirrors every dot and dash in exact synchrony with the flash — signal you can feel.
▤ TX
LIVE PREVIEW
Scroll through the full Morse pattern of your message before transmitting. Active character glows in real time.
◉ RX
CAMERA DECODING
Samples camera brightness 12× per second via a low-quality JPEG heuristic. No pixel decoding, no latency.
◈ RX
TWO-STEP CALIBRATION
Dark baseline + bright baseline → midpoint threshold. Works across any device, any lighting environment.
≡ APP
IN-APP GUIDE
Swipeable 6-slide tutorial covering TX and RX. Always accessible via the ? button — no internet required.
